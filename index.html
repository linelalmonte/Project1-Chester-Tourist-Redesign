Discover Chester’s Must-See Highlights

Embark on a journey through the charming streets of Chester, where history and modernity blend seamlessly. From its ancient Roman walls to picturesque Tudor-style buildings, Chester offers a captivating array of attractions waiting to be explored. Stroll through the vibrant Rows, marvel at the grandeur of Chester Cathedral, and unwind along the serene River Dee. Whether you're a history buff, a nature enthusiast, or a lover of unique shopping and dining experiences, Chester’s must-see highlights promise unforgettable moments for every traveler.

Chester Zoo

Discover one of the UK’s most renowned zoos, home to over 20,000 animals. A perfect day out for families and animal lovers alike.

Historic Chester Walls

Explore the ancient city walls that have stood the test of time. Enjoy panoramic views of Chester and stroll along these historic fortifications.

Grosvenor Museum

Marvel at the stunning Gothic architecture of Chester Cathedral. Experience its serene atmosphere and rich history.

The Rows

Step back in time with Chester’s unique medieval shopping galleries. Enjoy a blend of history and modern retail therapy.

Roman Amphitheatre

Witness the remnants of Chester’s Roman past at the amphitheatre, where gladiatorial contests and public events once took place.

Chester Cathedral

Marvel at the stunning Gothic architecture of Chester Cathedral. Experience its serene atmosphere and rich history.
